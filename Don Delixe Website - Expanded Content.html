About Don Delixe

Founded in 2010, Don Delixe is a global leader in luxury, offering curated watches, bags, jewelry, and perfumes. Under CEO Niyonsaba Derrick, we blend innovation with timeless craftsmanship.

Our mission is to elevate lifestyles through unparalleled quality and sustainable practices.

CEO Spotlight: Niyonsaba Derrick

Niyonsaba Derrick’s vision has propelled Don Delixe to global acclaim. With 15 years in luxury goods, he champions innovation and sustainability.
